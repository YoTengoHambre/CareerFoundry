More About Me

I have previously done one-off development projects in my time as a systems administrator, I have devoted my last year to become a web developer and am looking to join a team full time.

Why did you decide to become a web developer?

There is an inherent joy in creating something. Web development is satisfying in particular because it marries technical mastery with artistic inclination. The thrill of taking an idea and making it a reality is what drives me most.

What is your design process?

First, I assess what actually needs to be accomplished technically and how that can be done, and once I have the basic logic in place I experiment with the user interface and usability. To find the best visually user intuitive design that is satisfying to the end user, I like to experiment with multiple mock designs as I hone in on what works for a specific site or app.

What is your project turn around time?

It varies with each project and my current workload, which is why you should enquire to get the most accurate estimate.
